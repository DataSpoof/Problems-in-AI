# Energy and Compute Problem in AI

The **Energy and Compute Problem** in AI refers to the challenge of:

> The enormous computational power and electricity required to train and run modern AI systems.

In simple words:

- advanced AI needs huge hardware,
- massive GPUs,
- enormous data centers,
- and very high energy consumption.

As AI models grow larger,
the cost of computation grows rapidly.

---

# Simple Real-Life Example

Imagine teaching:

- one student in a classroom.

Easy and cheap.

Now imagine teaching:

- millions of students simultaneously,
- 24 hours a day,
- using supercomputers.

That resembles modern AI training.

---

Modern AI systems require:

- massive matrix computations,
